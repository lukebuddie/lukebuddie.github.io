Hello!

I'm Luke Buddie, an Economics student building a foundation in finance, strategy, and data.

Connect on LinkedIn View my Resume

Four years at Berkeley has meant competing at the Division I level while staying deeply invested in business and entrepreneurship. As a Decathlete and two-time Team Captain, I've learned to manage time, perform under pressure, and lead — not just train. Outside the track, I've studied Economics with a focus on finance and data, worked across sports media, operations, and business development, and built programs connecting student-athletes to industry. I'm drawn to the intersection of quantitative rigor and real-world impact.

I'm most excited about early-stage companies at the intersection of venture capital, health and consumer tech, AI, and sports. I think the next wave of interesting businesses gets built where those spaces overlap — and I want to be in the room early. I'm actively looking for opportunities in venture and growth-stage finance as I head into a Master's in Commerce at UVA McIntire. When I'm not running through spreadsheets or chasing a PR, I'm on an endless search for my next rec league basketball team and a run club that doesn't leave me in the dust.
