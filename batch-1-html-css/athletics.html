A complete approach to athletics.

The Decathlon is the ultimate test of a complete athlete — 100m, long jump, shot put, high jump, and 400m on day one; 110m hurdles, discus, pole vault, javelin, and 1500m on day two. It demands speed, strength, technique, and the mental fortitude to compete again after every event.

Balancing 20+ hours of weekly training with full-time coursework has sharpened my time management, resilience, and ability to perform under pressure — skills I carry into every professional and academic context.
